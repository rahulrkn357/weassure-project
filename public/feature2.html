Insurance E-Cards

What is an insurance E-Card?

An E-Card is your digital identity card for your insurance TPA. It proves that you are registered under the TPA and eligible for benefits.
When you show this card at the hospital:

  • ● The hospital identifies you as a policyholder
  • ● It sends a request to the TPA for cashless treatment process

How to download the E-Card?

Your E-Card contains basic informations like:

  • ● Employee Name
  • ● Employee ID
  • ● Date of Birth
  • ● Age/Gender
  • ● Mobile No
  • ● UHID (unique health ID exclusive to the TPA)
  • ● Corporate Name
  • ● Insurance Name (at the top)
  • ● Policy Number
  • ● Policy Period

How to download the E-Card?

E-Card can be downloaded easily using the given steps:

  • ● Go to the Dashboard
  • ● Click on “Download E-Card”
  • ● Choose any one method to download:- Enter UHID + Date of Birth OR Enter Employee ID + Phone Number
  • ● Enter the values and a preview of the E-Card will be displayed like in the example given below:
  • ● The E-Card will be saved as a PDF under employee ID

Remember:

Always check whether your E-Card is valid and up-to-date before visiting the hospital. An expired or incorrect E-Card can lead to claim rejections and
payment hassles later.
