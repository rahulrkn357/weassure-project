Inception: The company was founded to manage health insurance claims and services. Operations began with a small network of hospitals and partners.
Early Growth: The company expanded its hospital network and partnerships with insurers. Claim services became faster and more organized.
Expansion: Operations grew across more regions with more hospitals joining the network. The company handled a larger number of claims.
Digital Development: New digital systems were introduced to improve claim processing. This helped make services quicker and more efficient.
COVID-19 Period: During the COVID-19 pandemic, the company supported hospitals and policyholders with claim services. Despite challenges, operations continued smoothly.
Continued Growth: The company focuses on better technology and faster claim services. It continues to expand partnerships with hospitals and insurers.
